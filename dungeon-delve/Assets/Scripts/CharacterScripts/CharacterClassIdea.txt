Required fields:
Health
Damage
Speed
Ability, Implemented as a separate component to attack
IAttack interface, Get component

Required for hero:
Classes

Attacks come from other characters(damage), after a certain time (speed), characters take damage (health)

Attacks have specific behaviors based on abilities, Like 40% of the enemies

Reciving damage can have a specific behavior based on abilities, damage reduction, thorns

Battle manager fires a tick event after a set amount of time (this can be changed with the ui buttons), 
all characters advance towards their attack based on their speed stat (1000/speed),
When a character exceeds their speed, they attack the first member of the oppositions party using an attack interface
the attacked character calls a damage function
